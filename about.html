Our Story

Founded in 2018, DigSkills began as a small initiative to address the growing digital skills gap in Nigeria. What started as weekend coding workshops has now grown into a premier digital education hub with Cisco Networking Academy accreditation.

We recognized that traditional education systems weren't keeping pace with technological advancements, leaving both individuals and institutions struggling to adapt. Our mission became clear: to provide accessible, industry-relevant digital education that empowers people and transforms organizations.

Today, we've trained over 8,000 students and partnered with 200+ institutions across West Africa, helping them navigate their digital transformation journeys.

Our Approach

At DigSkills, we believe in learning by doing. Our programs combine theoretical knowledge with hands-on, project-based learning using real-world scenarios.

We've developed a unique "Skills Stack" methodology that layers technical skills with essential workplace competencies like problem-solving, collaboration, and adaptability.

Our Cisco-certified instructors bring years of industry experience, ensuring our curriculum remains relevant to current market demands. We continuously update our programs based on feedback from our employer network and technological advancements.

Our Cisco Networking Academy Partnership

As an official Cisco Networking Academy partner since 2020, we deliver world-class networking and cybersecurity education using Cisco's globally recognized curriculum.

This partnership allows us to offer:

  • Industry-standard course materials and virtual labs
  • Preparation for globally recognized Cisco certifications
  • Access to Cisco's global learning community
  • Regular instructor training and updates

Our students benefit from the same curriculum used to train networking professionals worldwide, with localized support from our experienced instructors.
