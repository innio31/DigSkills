Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our website development services.

What's included in the subscription fee?

Your subscription includes website hosting, security updates, technical support, and regular content updates (up to 5 pages per month). We also provide analytics reports to track your website's performance.

How long does it take to set up my school website?

Typically, we can have your website live within 2-3 weeks after receiving all necessary content and information from your institution. The exact timeline depends on the complexity of your requirements and how quickly we receive your materials.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. We'll prorate the difference in cost and seamlessly migrate your website to the new plan without any downtime.

Do you provide training for staff?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training for your staff on how to manage and update your website's content. For Premium plans, we also offer training on using the student portal features.

What happens if I want to cancel?

You can cancel your subscription at any time. We'll provide you with all your website files and data, and help transition to another provider if needed. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
